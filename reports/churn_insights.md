# 📞 Customer Churn Analysis – Insights Report

## 📌 Overview

This report summarizes key insights derived from the **Telecom Customer Churn Dataset** after performing data cleaning, feature engineering, and exploratory data analysis.

The goal is to understand **customer behavior patterns** and identify factors contributing to churn.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Month-to-Month Contracts Drive Higher Churn

Customers with **month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn rate compared to long-term contracts.

👉 Customers without commitment are more likely to leave.

---

### 2. High Monthly Charges Increase Churn Risk

Customers with **higher monthly charges** are more likely to churn.

👉 Pricing plays a critical role in customer retention.

---

### 3. Low Tenure Customers Churn More

Customers with **short tenure (new customers)** show a higher tendency to churn.

👉 Early customer experience is crucial.

---

### 4. Fiber Optic Users Show Slightly Higher Churn

Customers using **fiber optic internet services** have slightly higher churn rates.

👉 Possible reasons:
- Higher cost
- Service expectations not met

---

### 5. Paperless Billing is Associated with Higher Churn

Customers using **paperless billing** tend to churn more frequently.

👉 These customers may be more digitally active and flexible to switch providers.

---

## 📊 Business Interpretation

These insights highlight critical areas for telecom companies:

- Improve onboarding experience for new customers  
- Offer incentives for long-term contracts  
- Review pricing strategies  
- Improve service quality for high-value users  

---

## 🎯 Recommendations

- Introduce **loyalty programs** for long-term customers  
- Provide **discounts for annual contracts**  
- Monitor high-paying customers closely  
- Improve customer support for fiber users  

---

## ✅ Conclusion

Customer churn is influenced by **contract type, pricing, tenure, and service usage**.

By leveraging these insights, companies can take **data-driven actions** to reduce churn and improve customer retention.
