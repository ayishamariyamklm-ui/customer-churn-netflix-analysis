# Business vs Entertainment Data Analysis

## Customer Churn (Business Data)

- Focus: Customer retention
- Type: Structured data
- Goal: Predict churn
- Output: Business decisions

---

## Netflix Data (Entertainment Data)

- Focus: Content trends
- Type: Semi-structured data
- Goal: Understand viewer preferences
- Output: Strategic insights

---

## Key Learning

Business datasets require **predictive analysis**, while entertainment datasets focus on **trend analysis**.
